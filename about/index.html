About Me

I’m Jared Komoroski, a happy husband, and proud father to two little girls. Professionally, I’m an aspiring SDET at Alianza. I live in Eagle Mountain, Utah near the Silicon Slopes tech hub. I’m a graduate (B.A.) of Southern Utah University, where I studied the German Language and English Literature. My degree reinforced my love of histories, biographies, and fiction. In my spare time, I enjoy working on my 1965 VW Beetle with my girls, and watching too much Star Trek.
